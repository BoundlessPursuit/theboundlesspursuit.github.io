Our Story

A couple with a craving for adventure, culture, and the kind of memories that stay with you forever.

"The best journeys leave you with more than memories. They leave you with a deeper understanding of the world and your place within it."

Meet Annie & Ian

Two explorers. One shared passion.

We're Annie and Ian — a husband and wife team from Southern California with a passion for adventure, exceptional food, and discovering what makes every corner of the world uniquely beautiful.


Ian grew up in San Diego and has always been drawn to the outdoors. From viewing the Himalayan peaks in Nepal, hiking through Switzerland, exploring underground tunnels in New Zealand, or surfing in Nicaragua and Indonesia, he has always sought experiences that push him beyond the familiar.


Annie grew up among the beach cities of Los Angeles. She has always been drawn to the details that make a destination unforgettable — a meal that tells the story of a place, a neighborhood that no guidebook could ever fully capture. From the street food markets of Thailand to the colorful streets of Colombia and the distinctive hotels of Europe, she brings a discerning eye for culture, connection, and genuine hospitality to every journey she plans.

Annie, Ian & Dustin — Balboa Park, San Diego

How It Began

Born from a love of the journey itself

Long before Boundless Pursuit existed, we were the friends planning the group trips, researching the hidden restaurants, and finding the routes that took us somewhere unexpected. We realized that unforgettable journeys rarely happen by accident. They are built through thoughtful planning, local insight, and a willingness to look beyond the obvious. What began as a personal passion, evolved into a planning philosophy.

Our travels have created memories that continue to shape us — from dancing in Cuba and exploring life along the Mekong River in Vietnam and Cambodia to hiking glaciers in Patagonia. We chose to celebrate our marriage in Costa Rica, bringing together friends and family for a destination experience that reflected our shared love of travel. Those experiences reinforced a simple belief: the most meaningful travel experiences are the ones that immerse you in a destination and stay with you long after you return home. That belief became Boundless Pursuit.

Life Beyond Travel

The things that fuel us

Boundless Pursuit is the natural extension of who we are. Every trip we plan, carries the same intention we bring to our own travel — thoughtful, personal, and moments worth remembering.

Skiing & Snowboarding
The mountain is where we feel most alive. From groomed runs to backcountry powder, winter sport is a core part of who we are — and something we love helping clients discover for themselves.
Cycling & Staying Active
We're happiest when we're moving. Whether it's a sunrise bike ride along the coast or a trail that demands everything we've got, physical adventure is woven into how we travel and how we live.
Food, Wine & Hidden Gems
From hole-in-the-wall kitchens and street food markets to candlelit speakeasies and curated wine experiences — we are unapologetic foodies. If there's a secret door, a natural wine list, or a tasting menu worth talking about, we'll find it.
Dustin, Our Dog
Every great adventure needs a loyal companion. Dustin reminds us daily that joy is found in the simple things — and that no matter how far we roam, home is where the heart is.
Friends Who Are Family
We've built a community of people who show up for each other — the kind of friends who become family over time. That sense of connection is something we try to bring to every client relationship too.
Culture, History & Immersion
We travel to understand, not just to see. From ancient ruins to living traditions, we're drawn to the depth beneath the surface — and we design trips that invite clients to go deeper too.

Our Philosophy

Why we believe travel changes everything

We started Boundless Pursuit because we believe the world becomes a better place when more people truly experience it. Not just a visit — an experience. The kind of travel that humbles you, excites you, and sends you home different than when you left.


That's the standard we hold ourselves to. It's the kind of journey we want to help you create — wherever in the world that might take you.

"Travel offers one of life's greatest returns on investment. The experiences and perspectives gained along the way will stay with us for a lifetime. That's the kind of journey we hope to create for every client."

— Annie & Ian
