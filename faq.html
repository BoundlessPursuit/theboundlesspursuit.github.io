Working With Us

Working with Boundless Pursuit is a collaborative experience from start to finish. It begins with a conversation — either through our travel questionnaire or a direct message — where we get to know you, your travel style, your wishlist, and your budget.

From there, we take care of the research, planning, and booking so you don't have to. We handle the details, coordinate with hotels and suppliers, and stay in touch throughout your trip. Think of us as your personal travel partner — someone in your corner who genuinely cares about how the experience unfolds.

Online booking platforms are built for convenience — not for connection. They can't listen to your preferences, anticipate your needs, or advocate for you when something goes wrong.

We bring a human touch that no algorithm can replicate. As Virtuoso-affiliated advisors, we also have access to exclusive hotel benefits, preferred rates, room upgrades, and resort credits that are simply not available when you book direct or through an online travel agency. You often get more value working with us than booking on your own.

We earn a commission through our preferred hotel and supplier partnerships. For more complex itineraries — such as multi-destination trips, group travel, or highly detailed custom planning — a modest planning fee may apply.

We are always upfront about this before any work begins, so there are never any surprises. Our goal is a partnership built on transparency and trust from the very first conversation.

Simply complete our travel questionnaire or send us a message directly at travel@theboundlesspursuit.com. We will follow up within 48 hours to begin the conversation.

There is no obligation to book — we are happy to answer questions and explore ideas before any commitment is made.

Virtuoso Membership

Virtuoso is an invitation-only global network of luxury travel agencies and advisors. As Virtuoso-affiliated advisors, we have preferred relationships with some of the world's finest hotels, resorts, cruise lines, and tour operators.

For you, this means access to exclusive perks that are not available when booking directly — things like complimentary room upgrades, daily breakfast for two, resort credits, early check-in and late checkout, and VIP welcome amenities. These benefits are layered into your booking at no additional cost to you.
